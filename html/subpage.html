How To Make a Sandwich

  1. Take out the bread, lettuce, mayonnaise, and turkey from the refrigerator
  2. Spread the mayonnaise on the bread with a butter knife
  3. Put the turkey and lettuce on the pieces of bread
  4. Once put together you can eat it

    5. How To Make a Pupusa

    1. Take the masa and make it into a ball in your hands
    2. Then flatten it out on the table
    3. Put whatever ingredients on it; ex (frijoles, cheese, or meat)
    4. Roll it up again into a ball and flatten it
    5. Finally put it on a pan and keep an eye on it, wait until one side is cooked and flip it to the other
