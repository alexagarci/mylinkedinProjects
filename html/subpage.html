How To Make a Pupusa

  1. Take the masa and make it into a ball in your hands
  2. Then flatten it out on the table
  3. Put whatever ingredients on it; ex (frijoles, cheese, or meat)
  4. Roll it up again into a ball and flatten it
  5. Finally put it on a pan and keep an eye on it, wait until one side is cooked and flip it to the other side
